## 2. Key Strategic Insights

### 2.1 Product-Keyword Fit

The site's core product — a 14-day organized trip to Vietnam and Cambodia in Spanish — maps directly to high-intent keywords:
- **"viaje vietnam y camboya"** = 1,000/mo (Spain) — exact product match
- **"viaje organizado vietnam"** = 260/mo — describes the service model
- **"viaje a vietnam todo incluido"** = 110/mo (Spain) + 30/mo (Mexico) — describes the pricing model
- **"viaje vietnam 15 dias"** = 210/mo — matches the 14-day duration

### 2.2 Destination Name Volume Gap

Destination names command massive search volume but are purely informational. The site mentions all destinations but doesn't optimize content for these searches. This is the largest volume opportunity via content marketing (blog articles).

### 2.3 Spain vs LATAM Markets

Spain dominates Spanish-language search volume for Asia travel keywords (5-10x higher than any LATAM country). Mexico is the second market. The site's audience (Latin Americans) searches less for these terms, suggesting:
- The Spanish market is underserved and highly valuable
- LATAM audience discovers the trip via social media (Instagram), not Google
- SEO strategy should prioritize Spain as the primary organic growth market

### 2.4 Seasonal Patterns

"Viaje a vietnam" peaks in January/March (trip planning season) and dips in December. "Viaje a vietnam agosto" shows seasonal intent — people search for summer travel dates. Both match the August 2026 and April 2027 departure windows.
